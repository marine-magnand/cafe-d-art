ABOUT US

Who are we?

Café d'Art is not just a coffee shop; it's an innovative project designed to provide customers with an enriching, instagrammable and aesthetically pleasing environment.

Ideal for spending quality time with friends, family, or a special someone while visiting the vibrant city of Paris, our concept store aims to offer a hybrid experience and to be a place for creativity, where clients can indulge in a unique blend of brunching and artistic expression.

The heart of our concept lies in offering customers the opportunity to express their creativity through adding a personalised touch on various objects during their visit.

The ambiance at Café d'Art is carefully maintained, featuring different atmospheres and colors that will change every four months, creating a dynamic and ever-evolving experience for our guests.
